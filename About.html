About

About Ms. Pall

I am a High School Chemistry teacher at Colonel By Secondary School in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. I teach chemistry to the enriched 3UE (equivalent to Grade 11, or the 'GCSE' program in England); and the OAC/4UE, (equivalent to the Grade 12 program, or the 'A' Level in England), at Colonel By.

I also contributed in establishing the International Baccalaureate (IB) Chemistry Program at Colonel By. I designed the IB Chemistry program to align with the Ontario Curriculum in order to comply it with the 4 year high school program in Ontario.

I am an acitve member of the Chemical Institute of Canada; participating in promoting and providing opportunity for chemistry in the region. Furthurmore, I have been involved in many projects such as: "Speaker at the Careers Night for Students" at both Ottawa and Carleton University, a Judge for the "Regional Science Fair", as well as orgaining the "First National Chemistry Olympics for CIC".

On the side, I have trained students for the Avogadro , CHEM13 , CIC , CCO, First Chemistry National in Ottawa Contest, and the MCAT.

About Website

This website was created in order to provide accesible, quality resources for the chemistry education program. These materials are meant for Colonel By high school students; it includes notes, labs, assignments and review packages. These resource materials are freely to be used.
